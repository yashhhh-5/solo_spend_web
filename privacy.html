Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Privacy First

Welcome to Solo Spend.

Solo Spend is designed with one simple principle:

Your financial information belongs to you.

Unlike many expense tracking applications, Solo Spend works completely offline. The app does not require an account, does not use cloud storage, and does not collect or transmit your personal information.

Everything you record remains stored on your own device unless you choose to export it yourself.

Information We Collect

Solo Spend does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The application does not require:

  • Creating an account
  • Signing in
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Name
  • Address
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Banking information
  • Payment information

Expense Data

All expense records, budgets, categories, notes, and spending history are stored locally on your device using local storage.

Solo Spend does not upload, synchronize, or back up your information to any server or cloud service.

We cannot access, view, or recover your expense data.

PDF Export

Solo Spend allows you to export your expense history as PDF reports.

PDF files are generated entirely on your device.

The exported files are stored or shared only if you choose to do so. Solo Spend does not receive, upload, or store exported reports.

Notifications

If reminder notifications are enabled, Solo Spend may display local notifications on your device to remind you to record expenses or stay on track with your budget.

These notifications are created and managed entirely on your device.

Solo Spend does not use any servers, cloud services, or remote systems to deliver notifications.

Internet Connection

Solo Spend is designed to function completely offline.

An internet connection is not required to use the application\'s features.

Third-Party Services

Solo Spend does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services to collect, process, or store your personal information.

Data Security

Your expense information remains stored locally on your device.

To help protect your information, we recommend securing your device using a password, PIN, fingerprint, or Face ID.

Because your data never leaves your device, you remain in full control of your information.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data.

You can permanently remove all stored information at any time by:

  • Clearing the application\'s storage from your device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the application.

Once deleted, the information cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

Solo Spend is intended for general use.

Because the application does not collect, transmit, or store personal information, no personal data from children or any other users is collected.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements or changes to the application.

Any updates will be published on this page with a revised Last Updated date.
