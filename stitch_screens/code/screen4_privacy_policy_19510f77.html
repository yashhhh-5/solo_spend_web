Privacy Policy

Solo Spend is engineered with a radical commitment to data sovereignty. Our privacy policy is simple because our architecture is private by design.

database

Data Sovereignty

At the core of Solo Spend is the Hive database. Unlike conventional financial apps that sync your sensitive transactions to the cloud, Solo Spend operates strictly on-device.

Every expense, category, and budget you create is encrypted and stored within your device's secure local storage. Your financial identity never leaves your physical possession. We do not have servers, and we do not have access to your keys.

signal_wifi_off

No Internet Permissions

Transparency is our ultimate security feature. Solo Spend is built to function entirely offline.

The application binary does not request `android.permission.INTERNET` or similar networking capabilities. This architectural constraint ensures that even if there were a vulnerability, the app has no physical pathway to transmit data across the web.

visibility_off

Third-Party Access

Solo Spend does not integrate with third-party analytics, crash reporting tools like Firebase or Sentry, or advertising SDKs.

We believe that "anonymized" tracking is still tracking. By removing all external dependencies, we eliminate the risk of third-party data leaks and background profiling. Your usage patterns are your business alone.

folder_shared

User Control

You own your data. Solo Spend provides robust tools for you to manage your information:

  • Export to PDF: Generate professional financial summaries for your own records or tax purposes.
  • Local Backups: Export your Hive database encrypted file to move between devices manually.
  • Nuke Option: A single-tap "Delete All Data" function that wipes the local database and cache instantly.
